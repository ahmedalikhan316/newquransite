Courses

Learn Quran for kids

Through “Learn Quran for Kids” online course, your kids will understand the regulations and rules set by Allah (SWT) and use them as guidelines to follow throughout their lives.

Quran recitation with Tajweed

This course helps you to learn Quran recitation with Tajweed. Our excellent Quran tutors start with the students step-by-step to help them reach a very good level in reciting the holy Quran applying tajweed rules.

Quran Memorisation

Quran Memorization, especially with tajweed, involves physical, mental, and spiritual journey that will build a better relationship between an individual and Allah (SWT), as well as between an individual and community.

Tafseer & Translation

Quran Tafseer & Translation help Muslims understand the Holy Quran even better. As it helps them to interpret the literal as well as the technical meaning of the Quran verses.

Learn Arabic Language

This course will help you to learn the Arabic language and be able to communicate, write, and read Arabic very easily. You or your kids will learn verbs and make perfect sentences with no grammar mistakes.

Islamic Studies

This course will help you or your kids to learn various Islamic studies including but not limited to pillars of Islam, Fiqh, Aqidah, Tafsir Quran, Hadith, Dua (Supplication), Islamic Manners, and Seerah of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).
